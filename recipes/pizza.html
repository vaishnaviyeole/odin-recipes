Pizza

Description

Pizza is a delicious baked dish topped with sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings.

It is one of the most popular foods in the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll out the dough.
  3. Add sauce and toppings.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve.
