Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with tortillas filled with meat, vegetables, and sauces.

They are quick to prepare and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook the beef.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Add fillings to tortillas.
  4. Top with cheese and salsa.
  5. Serve immediately.
