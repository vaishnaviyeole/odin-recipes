Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and rich seasonings.

It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
